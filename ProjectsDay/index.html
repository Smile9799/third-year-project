How does it work?

Innovator

  • You register on our mobile application or website as an innovator.
  • We verify you after doing our necesary checks
  • You put an application for your investment as a business pitch on any of our platforms.
  • The pitch gets reviewed, by our trained proffessional reviewers, if it meets what the investors are interested it is then pushed to the investors
  • An innovator sees yoiur business pitch and sets up a meeting with you.
  • You meet with the investor through our embedded video call feature, to avoid the spread of the COVID virus.
  • The investor puts a bid for your business pitch.
  • You accept or counter the pitch.
  • Once both parties agree, you are business partners.
